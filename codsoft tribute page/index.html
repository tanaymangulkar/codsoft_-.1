Introduction

MS Dhoni, born on July 7, 1981, is a former Indian cricketer and captain who is widely regarded as one of the greatest finishers in limited-overs cricket. He led the Indian cricket team to numerous victories, including the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011.

Career Highlights

  • ICC Cricket World Cup Winner: 2011
  • ICC Champions Trophy Winner: 2013
  • ICC T20 World Cup Winner: 2007
  • Padma Bhushan Award: 2018

Retirement

MS Dhoni announced his retirement from international cricket on August 15, 2020, ending a glorious career that spanned over 15 years.
